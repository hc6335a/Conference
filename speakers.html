December 10th, 2019 at the National Building Museum

As technology continues to progress and become an integral part of daily life, we must create innovative design that allows for effortless usability. Design is the means by which the user experiences technolgy, and must span all abilities and interfaces. For one day, we have brought together some of the brightest minds in design and tech to discuss the future of usable design.
